Who We Are

Our main goal is to reach 75 Tonnes daily production volume by 2018, and become the largest scale manufacturer in our region. In addition, by closely following the global world in terms of technology and service, first class production and first class service will be at the top of our priorities. Our greatest capitals to achieve these objectives are not other than our work desires and wishes. Our facility, with a total area of 40.000 m2, 13.000 m2 of which is closed area, has been designed in order to carry out production in extremely hygienic conditions.
